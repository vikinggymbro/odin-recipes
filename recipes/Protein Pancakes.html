Banana Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Protein Pancakes

Description

These protein packed pancakes make a great post-workout meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 bananas, brokein into chunks
  • 1 1/2 cups dairy free milk
  • 6 eggs (or substitute with 18 tablespoons eggwhites)
  • 1/2 cup monkfruit sweetner
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup dairy free chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon avocado oil (as needed)

Steps

  1. Blend oats into oat flour. Mix oat flour, flour, protein powder, and cinnamon together in a bowl.
  2. Place bananas, milk, eggs, monkfruit sweetner, and vanilla extract in a blender; blend until smooth. Slowly add oats mixture to banana mixture while blender is runnning, until batter is smooth.
  3. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium-low heat; add avocado oil. Pour batter over hot oil, about 1/4 cup per pancake; dot with chocolate chips. Cook until pancakes begin to bubble and edges are dry, 3 to 5 minutes. Flip and cook until other side is done, 3 to 5 minutes more.
