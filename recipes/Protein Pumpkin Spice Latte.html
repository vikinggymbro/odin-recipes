Protein Pumpkin Spice Latte

Description

This sugar free, high protein PSL is a great replacement to your favorite fall starbucks beverage. Packed with protein and caffiene this is a great to start the day!

Ingredients

  • 1 Premier Protein (Pumpkin Spice Flavored)
  • 3 shots of espresso
  • 2 tablespoon pumpkin cheesecake skinny syrup
  • 2 teaspoons monkfruit sweetner

Steps

  1. Brew your espresso.
  2. Meanwhile put the skinny syrup, and monkfruit sweetner in your coffee cup.
  3. Use a milk frother to heat up and froth the premier protein shake.
  4. Add the fresh brewed espresso to the coffee cup.
  5. Pour the frothed protein shake into the coffee cup and gently stir.
  6. Enjoy!
